# Privacy Policy for CreatorX

**Last Updated**: January 26, 2026

## Introduction

CreatorX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information**: Username, niche/industry, subscriber count (for creators), company name (for brands)
- **Content Data**: Scripts, titles, thumbnails, captions, and other content you generate using our AI tools
- **Personas**: Audience profiles, brand voice definitions, and character descriptions you create
- **Feedback**: Ratings, comments, and feedback you provide on generated content

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Features used, pages visited, time spent, actions taken
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
- **Analytics Data**: App performance, crash reports, error logs
- **Network Information**: Connection type, online/offline status

### Permissions (Mobile Apps)

- **Camera**: To capture photos for thumbnail generation (optional)
- **Photo Library**: To select images from your device (optional)
- **Notifications**: To send you updates about content generation and collaborations (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
- Generate AI-powered content based on your inputs
- Personalize your experience and recommendations
- Communicate with you about your account and Service updates
- Send push notifications (if you opt-in)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
- Comply with legal obligations

## AI and Machine Learning

- Your content inputs are used to generate AI responses using third-party AI services (OpenAI, Google Vertex AI, Groq)
- We do not train AI models on your personal data without explicit consent
- Generated content is stored on our servers for your access
- Feedback you provide helps improve content quality through reinforcement learning

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

### Service Providers

- **Cloud Hosting**: AWS, Google Cloud, or similar (server infrastructure)
- **AI Services**: OpenAI, Google Vertex AI, Groq (content generation)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Firebase (usage analytics)
- **Authentication**: Third-party OAuth providers (if applicable)

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

### Business Transfers

If CreatorX is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted password storage (bcrypt)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Generated Content**: Stored until you delete it or close your account
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 2 years
- **Backup Data**: May be retained for up to 90 days after deletion

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or push notifications
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@minimalthreads.in
